How we met

Lara and Menard's love story began in the most unexpected of places—on the digital campus of their college. They first crossed paths during an online event on Discord, just another virtual gathering to survive the monotony of online classes. Little did they know, this chance encounter would change their lives forever.

After the event, they added each other on Facebook, just friendly gestures at first. But behind the casual likes and comments, something deeper was brewing. Both harbored secret crushes, unaware of the other's feelings.

Then came that pivotal moment: Lara's Facebook story seeking a movie buddy. Despite his nerves, Menard took a leap of faith and asked her to hang out. To his utter surprise, she said yes.

From that moment on, their friendship blossomed into something more. They became inseparable, savoring every moment spent together. Whether it was lazy movie nights or spontaneous adventures, they found joy in each other's company.

Their love story may not have started with grand gestures or poetic proclamations, but it's real and genuine—a testament to the beauty of finding love in unexpected places. And now, as they embark on this new chapter together, they invite you to celebrate their journey and witness the magic of love.
